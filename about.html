About Me

My name is Zach Nickerson. I am a current student at Great Bay Community College as well as the University of New Hampshire, studying computer science. I have always had an interest in technology, starting at a very early age and never really deviating from it. I especially enjoy programming, which is my main focus but have experience in many different areas including web design and networking. There are a few things that made me want to pursue programming. I love the challenge and problem solving that comes with writing a program, and the satisfaction at the end when you finally get a program working just how you intended it to. I also beleive that the sky is the limit in terms of what you can create which makes for an always growing and worthwhile career.



I grew up in the Exeter area of New Hampshire, moving many times at a young age but always in the surrounding towns, and am the youngest out of three kids. I currently live in Brentwood, NH. Growing up I played a lot of sports, mainly baseball for years, but also basketball and bowling. In my free time you can find me either behind a computer screen working on a program or going out for some karaoke.
